## Interest vs Compounding interest:
i = interest rate
m = number of compounding periods in a year
t = time
**Static interest:**
FV = PV(1+i\*t)
**Compound:**
FV = PV(1+i/m)<sup>mt</sup> 

**Effective Annual Interest Rate:**
EAR= (1 + i/m)<sup>m</sup>-1
